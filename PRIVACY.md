# Privacy Policy for LatticeUI

Last updated: June 22, 2026

LatticeUI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the LatticeUI Home Launcher application.

## 1. No Personal Data Collection
LatticeUI does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information. The application runs entirely locally on your Android device.

## 2. On-Device Processing
All features, including the app drawer listing, search indexing, and usage-based app sorting, are computed dynamically on your device. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

## 3. Permissions Used
To function as a home screen launcher, LatticeUI may request certain permissions:
* **Usage Access / Query All Packages:** Used strictly to retrieve the list of installed apps on your device to display them in the app drawer.
* **Wallpaper Access:** Used to retrieve your current wallpaper image to render the custom glassmorphic blur effects.

None of the data accessed via these permissions is uploaded or transmitted off your device.

## 4. External Services
The application does not include any third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertisements.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/mathangandhi0/LatticeUI).
